# Glowishes Brand Voice

## Brand

Glowishes is a Greek K-beauty and cosmetics store.

## Voice

- Clean
- Premium
- Friendly
- Direct
- Modern
- Skincare educated, but not medical

## Default Language

Greek for customer-facing copy.

## Avoid

- Overpromising
- Medical claims
- Fake urgency
- Fake reviews
- Fake before-and-after claims
- Unsupported dermatologist or clinical claims

## Useful Phrases

- glow routine
- daily skincare
- lightweight feel
- hydrated look
- radiant look
- easy routine
