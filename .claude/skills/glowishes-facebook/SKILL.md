# Glowishes Facebook / Meta Skill

## Store context
Glowishes on Facebook — new page (few followers). Meta ecosystem = Facebook + Instagram (same Ads Manager). Primary Facebook value: Meta Ads reach + retargeting + Shopify catalog integration.

## Facebook algorithm (2025-2026)
Facebook organic reach for Pages is ~2-5% of followers. Algorithm is pay-to-play.
Organic content that still works:
1. **Video (Reels)** — cross-posts from Instagram, extra reach
2. **Groups** — community posts get 10x organic reach vs Page posts
3. **Events** — launch events, flash sales
4. **Local content** — geo-targeted posts (Athens, Thessaloniki, Kavala)
5. **Shares** — content people share to their friends

What the Facebook algorithm rewards:
- Comments (especially back-and-forth threads)
- Shares
- Reactions (Love/Care > Like)
- Video watch time
- Profile/Page visits after post

- Cross-post every Instagram Reel to Facebook Reels

## Post formats

### Facebook Reel (cross-post from Instagram)
- Same 9:16 video, same caption
- Add Facebook-specific hook in first line: "Αν ψάχνεις K-beauty στην Ελλάδα..."
- Facebook rewards native uploads over cross-posts when possible

### Long-form post (Facebook's strength vs Instagram)
```
First 2 lines: Hook (visible before "See More")
Story: 3-5 sentences with context and value
Product mention: natural, not forced
CTA: Comment, Share, or Link
Image: product or lifestyle photo
```

### Facebook Shop
- Sync Shopify catalog to Facebook Shop via Meta Commerce Manager
- Tag products in posts once Shop is approved
- This is a priority setup task

## Meta Ads structure

| Brand awareness | Reach | New product launch |

| Conversions | Conversions | After Pixel has 50+ events |

### Ad creative formats that work for K-beauty
- UGC-style video (Higgsfield Marketing Studio → UGC preset)
- Before/after concept (careful: Meta flags "before/after" health claims)
- Product demo Reel
- Testimonial-style (real or UGC-format scripted)
- Carousel: multi-product or multi-benefit

### Ad copy structure
```
Primary text (top):
Hook line (pain point or curiosity)
Value proposition (1-2 sentences)
Social proof if available

Headline (bold under image):
"[Product benefit] για κάθε δέρμα" or "Shop K-beauty στην Ελλάδα"

Description:
Short (optional): "Δωρεάν αποστολή άνω των X€"

CTA button: Shop Now / Learn More / Get Offer
```

## Compliance rules
Meta Ads are stricter than organic:
- No before/after images that imply medical transformation
- No "cure", "treat", "eliminate" language
- No claims about weight loss or body transformation
- Skincare education is safe; medical claims are not
- Cosmetic claims must be factual and supported

## Draft-only rule
Never post, schedule, launch ads, or spend budget. Drafts only. All Meta Ads require manual review and approval before launch.
