# Glowishes TikTok Skill

## Store context
Glowishes (@glowishes) — TikTok account in early growth phase (~15 followers). Goal: reach the FYP (For You Page), grow to 1K followers fast, drive traffic to glowishes.com.

## How the TikTok algorithm works (2025-2026)
TikTok distributes to small test pools first. Reach depends on:
1. **Completion rate** — most important metric. Video must be watched to the end.
2. **Rewatch rate** — videos watched multiple times signal quality.
3. **Shares** — strongest engagement signal.
4. **Comments** — especially early comments.
5. **Likes** — least important but still signals quality.

## What kills TikTok reach
- Boring first 0.5 seconds (instant scroll-off)
- Long intros ("Hey guys, welcome back...")
- Low energy / monotone delivery
- No text overlay (muted watching is common)
- Watermark from other apps (no CapCut watermarks on TikTok)
- Posting at wrong times

## Content strategy for <1K followers
- Post 1-2x per day
- Test different hooks aggressively
- Reply to EVERY comment (triggers more reach)
- Duet / Stitch trending skincare content
- Use trending sounds (check TikTok Creative Center)
- Shorter is better: 7-15 seconds outperforms 60-second content at this stage

## Script structure

### Hook (0-0.5s) — CRITICAL
Must immediately answer "why should I keep watching?"

Hook types that work for K-beauty:
- Challenge: "Βάζεις λάθος SPF? Σε λίγα δευτερόλεπτα το μαθαίνεις"
- Surprise: "Αυτό το Korean SPF κοστίζει λιγότερο από τον ελληνικό καφέ"
- Controversy: "Το ακριβό moisturizer σου δεν κάνει αυτό που νομίζεις"
- Secret: "Ο λόγος που η ρουτίνα σου δεν δουλεύει"
- List: "3 λάθη που κάνουν όλοι με το SPF"

### Full structure
```
0.0s:  Hook text overlay (bold, centered, 1-2 words visible)
0.5s:  Setup (problem or promise in 1-2 sentences)
3s:    Product reveal or action
8s:    Key benefit or insight (the "aha moment")
12s:   CTA ("Follow για περισσότερα" / "Link in bio")
```

### Voiceover vs text-only
- With face/creator: voiceover + text overlay
- Without creator: text overlay + trending audio (adjust volume to 30-50%)
